About Jellico

Jellico is a well-established accessories brand with international distribution across Asia, the Middle East, and Europe, particularly known for practical charging solutions at honest prices. At AdvanceStore we stock 52 Jellico products across 7 categories, priced from £10.99–£37.99.

What Jellico does well

Jellico built its reputation in charging accessories — and that focus shows. Their multi-tip cables (covering USB-C, Lightning, and Micro-USB from a single cable), PD charger sets, and power banks are consistently well-reviewed for build quality that outlasts their price point. The Jellico B and C cable series use reinforced connectors and braided sleeves similar to cables costing twice as much from premium brands.
