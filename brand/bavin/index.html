About BAVIN

BAVIN is a Philippines-originated accessories brand that has built strong distribution across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, and the UK. At AdvanceStore we stock 16 BAVIN products across 5 categories, priced from £13.99–£65.99.

BAVIN in the UK

BAVIN products are CE and RoHS certified for the UK market — the CE marking on BAVIN chargers confirms they meet UK and EU Low Voltage Directive and EMC requirements. All UK-destined BAVIN chargers use the standard UK 3-pin plug and are rated for 230V mains. BAVIN is particularly strong in three areas: wall chargers, TWS Bluetooth earphones, and power banks.

BAVIN product highlights

  • Wall chargers — compact single-port and dual-port models with UK plug, from 10W up to 65W PD. Often sold as charger + cable sets at bundle prices.
  • TWS earbuds — true wireless earbuds with ENC noise cancellation for calls, Bluetooth 5.1–5.3, battery life up to 30+ hours with case
  • Power banks — 10,000mAh and 20,000mAh models with PD20W output, built-in LED indicator
  • Car chargers — dual-port adapters with USB-C PD + USB-A QC3.0
  • Cables — nylon-braided USB-C, Lightning and Micro-USB cables in 1m and 2m lengths
