About HOCO

HOCO is one of the world's largest 3C mobile accessory brands, founded in 2009 in Shenzhen, China, and now distributed in over 150 countries. At AdvanceStore.co.uk we stock 1 genuine HOCO products across 1 categories, all priced from POA and carrying full CE and RoHS certification for the UK market.

Why HOCO?

HOCO stands for High Quality, Original, Creative and Outstanding — a brand philosophy that shows in how they engineer their products. Every HOCO charger uses a UK-standard 3-pin plug. Every cable is tested to more than 10,000 bend cycles. Every Bluetooth audio product undergoes 72-hour QC testing before leaving the factory. The result is a brand that competes with much more expensive accessories on quality while remaining accessible on price.

HOCO product series guide

  • C-series — wall chargers (C12, C76, C90 etc.) — single and dual-port, PD and QC variants
  • BX-series (BOROFONE sub-brand) — data and charging cables in silicone, nylon and standard variants
  • ES-series — Bluetooth earphones, TWS earbuds and in-ear wired earphones
  • W-series — over-ear headphones, wireless and wired
  • HC/BS-series — portable Bluetooth speakers
  • CA/CB-series — car chargers and in-car accessories
  • GM-series — computer mice and keyboards
  • K-series — phone holders for live streaming and desktop use
