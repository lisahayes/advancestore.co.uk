About BOROFONE

BOROFONE is the lifestyle sub-brand of HOCO Technology, launched to target a younger, design-conscious audience with bold colours, premium finishes, and accessible price points. At AdvanceStore we stock 84 BOROFONE products across 14 categories, priced from £10.99–£87.99.

BOROFONE vs HOCO — what's the difference?

Both brands share the same Shenzhen manufacturing infrastructure and quality control standards — the key difference is design language. HOCO products tend toward clean, professional aesthetics suited to workplace use. BOROFONE products are bolder, with more colour options, lifestyle-oriented packaging, and series names like "Graceful", "Kaya", "Lucy" and "Sound Dance" that reflect the brand's more expressive personality.

BOROFONE product series

  • BX-series — charging data cables; the most extensive cable range we carry, covering silicone, nylon-braided, and standard variants in every connector type
  • BJ-series — power banks from 5,000mAh to 30,000mAh
  • BR/BS-series — portable Bluetooth speakers
  • BL-series — digital audio adapters and AUX cables
  • BZ-series — wall chargers including UK-plug PD and QC variants
  • BE-series — gaming accessories including FPS trigger sets and controllers
