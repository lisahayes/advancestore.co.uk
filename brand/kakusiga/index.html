About Kakusiga (KSC)

Kakusiga is recognised by its KSC SKU system — every product carries a three or four-digit number prefixed with KSC (for example, KSC-970, KSC-1095). The brand has built a strong following in the UK for delivering reliable performance at prices significantly below premium brands like Belkin or Anker, while maintaining comparable build quality in most product categories.
