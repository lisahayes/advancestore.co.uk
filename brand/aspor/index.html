About Aspor

Aspor is a charging-focused accessories brand particularly popular across South and Southeast Asia, with a growing presence in UK import communities. At AdvanceStore we stock 31 Aspor products across 5 categories, priced from £10.99–£54.99.

Aspor's speciality: high-wattage cables

Aspor is best known for its A-series 100W USB-C cables — cables capable of charging laptops, not just phones. At a time when most budget cable brands cap out at 60W, Aspor's 100W cables serve the growing market of people who want to charge a MacBook or Dell XPS from a single USB-C hub without buying an expensive proprietary cable.

Aspor products at AdvanceStore

  • A-series cables — 27W Lightning, 60W USB-C to USB-C, 100W USB-C to USB-C; nylon-braided with aluminium alloy connectors
  • Multi-tip cables — 4-in-1 cables covering USB-C, Lightning, Micro-USB, and USB-A
  • Earphones — wired earphones for 3.5mm and USB-C connections with inline microphone

Build quality

Aspor cables use pure copper inner conductors (not aluminium-copper alloy, which is cheaper but has higher resistance), nylon braid rated for 15,000+ bends, and aluminium alloy shell connectors that don't crack or corrode. CE and RoHS certified for UK use.
