About Screen Protectors

A tempered glass screen protector is cheap insurance for an expensive display. At AdvanceStore we stock 7 screen protectors from £10.99 to £21.99 from Yesido. We cover iPhone, iPad, and camera lens protectors.

Tempered glass for iPhone

Our Yesido protectors carry a 9H hardness rating. This matches sapphire glass. Keys, coins, and most sharp objects won't scratch them. They bond to the screen with silicone adhesive and keep full touch sensitivity. Face ID still works. Our range covers:

  • iPhone 13, 13 Mini, 13 Pro, 13 Pro Max
  • iPhone 14, 14 Plus, 14 Pro, 14 Pro Max
  • iPhone 15, 15 Plus, 15 Pro, 15 Pro Max

Each protector includes a full installation kit: dust sticker, alcohol wipe, and alignment frame for bubble-free fitting. From £10.99.

iPad screen protectors

Yesido tempered glass for iPad models from 9.7 to 12.9 inches. Compatible with Apple Pencil — the stylus precision is not affected. From £15.99.

Camera lens protectors

Circular tempered glass discs for each camera lens on iPhone 15 models. A small nick in the lens glass noticeably affects photo quality. Our Yesido lens protectors are model-specific and come with adhesive rings for precise alignment. From £10.99.
