About Chargers & Wireless Charging

A fast charger can fill your phone in under an hour. At AdvanceStore we stock 85 UK-plug chargers and wireless charging solutions from £10.99 to £65.99.

USB-C Power Delivery chargers

Power Delivery (PD) is Apple's fast-charging standard. It works with every iPhone from iPhone 8 onwards. A PD20W charger fills an iPhone to 50% in about 30 minutes. Our PD30W chargers also fast-charge most Android phones and small tablets.

For MacBooks, iPads, and phones at once — try our 65W GaN chargers. GaN runs cooler than silicon. It fits in the same space as a 20W plug.

Quick Charge 3.0 chargers

QC3.0 is Qualcomm's standard. It charges Samsung, Xiaomi, and OnePlus phones up to 4x faster than a basic 5W plug. We stock QC3.0 models from Yesido, BOROFONE, Kakusiga, and Inkax.

Wireless charging pads

All our wireless chargers use the Qi standard. Compatible phones include iPhone 8 and later, Samsung Galaxy S8 and later, and all Google Pixel phones with wireless charging.

Our 3-in-1 wireless stations charge your phone, earbuds case, and Apple Watch at the same time. One cable, one surface, no clutter.

Which charger do you need?

  • iPhone users: PD20W minimum. PD30W if you also have a MacBook or iPad.
  • Android users: QC3.0 or PD30W. Check your phone spec for its maximum wattage.
  • Multiple devices: A GaN multi-port charger replaces several plugs.
