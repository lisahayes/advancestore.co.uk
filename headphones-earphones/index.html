About Audio – Headphones & Earphones

The right earphone depends on how you use it. At AdvanceStore we stock 76 audio products from £10.99 to £54.99. We cover true wireless earbuds, over-ear headphones, and wired earphones for every connector.

True Wireless Stereo (TWS) earbuds

TWS earbuds are the most popular type. No cables. Compact charging case. Bluetooth 5.1 to 5.4 for a stable signal. Our range includes models with Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) and Environmental Noise Cancellation (ENC) for clearer calls. Battery life runs 4–6 hours per charge. The case adds two to three more charges. Total battery: 20–40 hours. From £15.99.

Over-ear Bluetooth headphones

Better for long listening sessions. They offer more comfort and better passive noise blocking. Our models offer up to 46 hours wireless playback. Most include a 3.5mm AUX cable too. Useful on flights or gym equipment. From £21.99.

Wired earphones

No lag. No charging. Often better audio quality per pound than wireless at the same price. We stock:

  • 3.5mm jack — the universal option for laptops, tablets, and older phones
  • USB-C — for Android phones without a headphone jack
  • Lightning — for iPhone 7 through iPhone 14

All wired models include an inline microphone. From £10.99.

Which is right for you?

  • Commuting: TWS with ANC or over-ear with good passive isolation
  • Work calls: TWS with ENC mic, or a headset with a boom arm
  • Gym: TWS rated IPX4 or higher for sweat resistance
  • Travel: Over-ear with ANC and an included AUX cable for planes
