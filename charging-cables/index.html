About Charging Cables

The wrong cable can cut your charging speed in half. At AdvanceStore we stock 128 charging cables priced from £10.99 to £49.99. Every cable covers USB-C, Lightning, and Micro-USB. All are CE and RoHS certified for the UK.

USB-C cables

USB-C is now the standard for phones, tablets, and laptops. Our USB-C cables range from 60W for phones to 100W for laptops. Aspor A-series cables handle 100W. BOROFONE BX-series and Yesido CA-series cover everyday use.

Charging a MacBook or Samsung Galaxy Tab at full speed needs a cable rated for Power Delivery at 60W or higher. A cheap cable will throttle your charger.

Apple Lightning cables

For iPhone 14 and earlier, our Lightning cables support up to 27W PD fast charging. Brands include Yesido, BAVIN, Aspor, and Jellico. All have reinforced connectors that last.

3-in-1 multi-tip cables

One cable for USB-C, Lightning, and Micro-USB. Kakusiga and Jellico both make solid multi-tip options from £13.99. Great for travel or households with mixed devices.

How to choose

  • Wattage: Match your charger output. Most cheap cables cap at 10–15W even with USB-C connectors.
  • Length: 1m for desks, 2m for across a room, 0.25m for travel.
  • Nylon braid: Rated for 15,000+ bends vs 3,000 for standard PVC cables.
