About Live Streaming & Photography

Ring lights

A ring light removes shadows and gives even, flattering light. This is why it's become standard for video calls, beauty content, and product photography. Our LED ring lights range from compact 20cm desktop models to full-size 45cm floor versions. Look for: adjustable colour temperature (warm, neutral, cool), adjustable brightness, and a phone holder in the centre. From £21.99.

Tripods and phone holders

A stable tripod is the biggest quality upgrade you can make to your video. Our range covers mini tabletop tripods (15–25cm), full-size telescoping tripods (up to 170cm), and combined selfie stick tripods for travel. Universal clips fit all phones from 4.7 to 6.9 inches. From £15.99.

Selfie stick tripods

Extend for wide-angle shots. Open the legs to stand freely. Many include a Bluetooth remote shutter that works up to 10 metres away. From Yesido and BOROFONE from £21.99.

HD webcams

Sharper and wider than a laptop's built-in camera. Our Yesido 1080p HD webcams include autofocus and a built-in noise-cancelling microphone. Compatible with Zoom, Teams, OBS, and all major streaming platforms. Plug-and-play via USB. From £37.99.
