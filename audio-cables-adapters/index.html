About Audio Cables & Adapters

Missing headphone jacks, incompatible sockets, old car stereos — audio adapters fix all of these. At AdvanceStore we stock 15 audio cables and adapters from £10.99 to £21.99.

3.5mm AUX cables

The standard male-to-male AUX cable connects any device with a headphone socket to a speaker, amp, or car stereo. Available in 1m and 2m. Nylon-braided options for extra durability. BOROFONE and Yesido models from £10.99.

USB-C to 3.5mm adapters

For Android phones without a headphone jack. This includes Samsung Galaxy S21 and later, Google Pixel 6 and later, and OnePlus 7 and later. A DAC chip inside keeps the sound quality clean. From Yesido, BOROFONE, and Kakusiga from £10.99.

Lightning to 3.5mm adapters

For iPhone 7 through iPhone 14. Works with any standard 3.5mm headphones. The Yesido and BAVIN adapters include a DAC chip for clear audio output. From £12.99.

RCA cables

Dual-RCA to 3.5mm cables. Used to connect phones and laptops to home amplifiers and older TVs. Available in 1m and 2m. From £10.99.

Premium digital audio adapters

Our top Yesido adapters include a 24-bit DAC chip. They support up to 48kHz audio for cleaner sound. From £15.99.
