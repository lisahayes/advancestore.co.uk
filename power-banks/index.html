About Power Banks

A good power bank keeps you going when there's no socket in sight. At AdvanceStore we stock 31 portable chargers from £21.99 to £87.99. Brands include BOROFONE, Yesido, Jellico, and Kakusiga.

5,000mAh power banks

One full charge for most smartphones. These are slim — about the size of a credit card wallet — and weigh under 150g. Our BOROFONE BJ-series models start from £21.99. Some include a built-in cable. No separate cable to carry.

10,000mAh power banks

Two to three full phone charges. Still small enough for a bag. Look for a PD20W output port to charge your phone at full speed. Dual-output models charge two devices at once. From £26.99.

20,000–30,000mAh power banks

For weekend trips or long travel days. A 20,000mAh bank charges an average phone five or six times. Some models include a built-in LED torch. Useful for camping or power cuts. From £37.99.

50,000mAh power banks

For extended travel or charging several devices over days. Our largest Yesido YP-series bank supports PD22.5W output. It charges two devices at once. At £87.99 it's our premium option. Note: UK airlines allow power banks up to 100Wh in cabin bags. At 3.7V, 50,000mAh = 185Wh. This model must go in checked luggage on flights.

What to look for

  • Output wattage: PD20W minimum to fast-charge modern phones.
  • Input wattage: Higher input means faster recharging of the bank itself.
  • Number of ports: USB-C plus USB-A covers the widest range of devices.
