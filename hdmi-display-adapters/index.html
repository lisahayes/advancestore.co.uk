About HDMI & Display Adapters

One USB-C cable can connect your laptop, tablet, or phone to an external screen. At AdvanceStore we stock 12 HDMI and display adapters from £15.99 to £43.99. Brands include Yesido and Kakusiga.

USB-C to HDMI adapters

Connect any USB-C device to any HDMI monitor or TV. Output: up to 4K at 30Hz or 1080p at 60Hz. Compatible with MacBook, iPad Pro, iPad Air, Samsung Galaxy S and Tab, Dell XPS, and most USB-C laptops made after 2018. Gold-plated connectors for a reliable signal. From £15.99.

USB-C multi-port hubs with HDMI

Add HDMI output, extra USB-A ports, and USB-C passthrough charging — all from one adapter. Our Kakusiga and Yesido hubs let you work on an external display, use a mouse, and charge your laptop at the same time. From £26.99.

USB-C to VGA adapters

For projectors and older monitors that use VGA. Yesido VGA adapters support up to 1080p output. From £21.99.

Quick compatibility guide

  • MacBook (all USB-C models): USB-C to HDMI works natively
  • iPad Pro 2018+: Needs a USB-C to HDMI adapter
  • iPad Air 4th gen+: Needs a USB-C to HDMI adapter
  • Samsung Galaxy S8+: USB-C to HDMI with Samsung DeX support
