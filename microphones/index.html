About Microphones

A dedicated microphone makes a big difference to audio quality. At AdvanceStore we stock 13 microphones from £15.99 to £54.99. Brands include Yesido, BOROFONE, and Kakusiga.

Wireless lavalier microphones

Clip-on wireless mics give you freedom of movement. A small transmitter clips to your clothing. A receiver plugs into your phone's USB-C or Lightning port. No app needed. No Bluetooth pairing. Range up to 20 metres. The Yesido and BOROFONE wireless mics work directly with iPhone and Android. From £37.99.

USB desktop microphones

A desktop mic placed 20–30cm from your mouth sounds dramatically better than a laptop's built-in mic. Our Kakusiga and Yesido USB mics use a cardioid pickup pattern. This means they pick up your voice and reject background noise. Plug-and-play on Windows and Mac. From £21.99.

Dual wireless microphone sets

Two transmitters, one receiver. Both speakers recorded clearly and at the same time. Great for interviews, teaching, and two-person presentations. From £54.99.

Karaoke microphones

Wireless Bluetooth karaoke mics with built-in speakers. Connect to your phone, pick a track, and sing. Our Kakusiga and Yesido models support Bluetooth 5.0. They include echo and reverb effects. Battery lasts 3–5 hours. From £15.99.

Which mic do you need?

  • Vlogging / content: Wireless lavalier for freedom of movement
  • Podcasting / streaming: USB desktop for consistent quality
  • Interviews: Dual wireless set for two speakers
  • Casual / karaoke: Bluetooth karaoke mic
