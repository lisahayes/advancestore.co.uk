About USB Hubs, Card Readers & Storage

Modern laptops often have just one or two ports. Our range of 44 USB connectivity products from £8.99 to £43.99 adds everything you need.

USB-C multi-port hubs

Add four or more ports from a single USB-C connection. Our Yesido and Kakusiga hubs give you USB-A 3.0 (up to 5Gbps), USB-C data and power, HDMI or VGA output, and SD/TF card slots. All in a compact unit that fits in a pocket. From £21.99. Works with MacBook, iPad Pro, and any USB-C laptop.

SD and TF card readers

Transfer photos from a camera, drone, or dashcam. Our readers support SD, SDHC, SDXC (up to 2TB), and TF/microSD. Transfer speeds reach USB 3.0 at 5Gbps. Models available for USB-A and USB-C. Lightning card readers for iPhone also available. From £8.99.

OTG adapters

Plug USB flash drives, keyboards, or mice directly into your Android phone or tablet. The Yesido and Kakusiga USB-C OTG adapters are simple and plug-and-play. From £8.99.

USB-A hubs and flash drives

Four-port USB 2.0 and 3.0 hubs for desktops and laptops with USB-A ports. USB-A and USB-C flash drives in 32GB and 64GB from Yesido. From £10.99.

All products are plug-and-play on Windows 10+, macOS 11+, iPadOS 16+, and Android 8+. No drivers needed. Order on WhatsApp for same-day UK dispatch.
