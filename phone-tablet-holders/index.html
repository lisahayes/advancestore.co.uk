About Phone & Tablet Holders

A phone holder keeps your screen exactly where you need it. At AdvanceStore we stock 43 phone and tablet holders from £10.99 to £37.99. Brands include HOCO, Yesido, BAVIN, Kakusiga, and BOROFONE.

Car phone holders

Air vent mounts are the most popular type. They clip to your car vent, rotate 360°, and sit at roughly dashboard height. This is also the safest option — it doesn't block your view. Our HOCO, Yesido, and BAVIN vent holders fit all phones from 4.7 to 6.9 inches, including phones in cases. From £10.99.

Dashboard suction mounts attach to the windscreen or a smooth dashboard surface. More flexible placement. Our Kakusiga and Yesido models include anti-vibration joints that keep your phone steady on rough roads. From £12.99.

Wireless charging car holders combine a 15W Qi charger with the holder. Just place your phone to start charging — no cable needed. Our BOROFONE and Yesido models grip the phone automatically with motor-driven arms. From £21.99.

Important note on UK law

Using a phone without a holder while driving is illegal in the UK. It carries a £200 fine and 6 penalty points. A good holder protects your licence as well as your phone.

Bike and motorcycle holders

Handlebar mounts with rubber inserts and safety catches. Fits handlebars from 22mm to 35mm. From £10.99.

Desktop phone stands

Adjustable-angle stands for home working, recipes, or watching content. Many fold flat for storage. From £10.99.
